Creamy Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

This is one of the dishes that I enjoy as my guilty pleasure as a huge fan of potatoes myself.

I usually enjoy eating this with some western mains.

Ingredients

  • 2kg Potatoes, peeled
  • 3 to 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 10 tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2.5 cups warm heavy cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into quarters or halves depending on sizes.
  2. Place potatoes in a large pot and pour just enough cold water to cover potatoes. Add garlic if you’d like to and 1 tbsp salt.
  3. Cook potatoes until chopstick tender, about 15 to 18 minutes over high heat.
  4. Drain cooked potatoes and rice them using a potato ricer for the silky smooth mashed potatoes.
  5. Add butter, heavy cream and 2 tsp salt. Mix everything well. Add extra 1/3 cup of warm heavy cream if you’re not serving immediately. Serve with extra butter and black pepper to your taste. Enjoy!
